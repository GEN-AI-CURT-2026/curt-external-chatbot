# CURT Achievements and Competition History

## Competition Participation History

CURT has participated a total of **8 times** in Formula Student competitions since 2012:

### 2012 - Formula Student UK (First Participation)
- **Historic Milestone:** First Egyptian team and first team in MENA Region to participate in Formula Student
- Competed with an ICE (Internal Combustion Engine) car
- Location: Silverstone Circuit, UK

### 2015 - Formula Student Germany
- **Achievement:** Won the "Most Challenging Award"
- Competed with ICE car

### 2016 - Formula Student UK & Germany
- **Achievement:** Successfully passed scrutineering in both competitions
- First Egyptian/African team to pass all technical inspections in UK and Germany
- Competed with ICE car

### 2017 - Formula Student Italy
- **Major Achievement:** 3rd Place in Cost Event
- **Historic Milestone:** First African team to reach dynamic events in Germany (also in 2017)
- Competed with ICE car

### 2018 - Last ICE Car
- **Achievement:** Ranked 17th in Cost Event
- Final competition with internal combustion engine

### 2019 - First Electric Vehicle (EV)
- **Historic Milestone:** First Egyptian team to compete with electric vehicle
- **Major Achievement:** 11th place in Cost and Manufacturing Event
- **Regional Excellence:** 1st in MENA for electric car design
- **Global Recognition:** 5th place globally among EV participants at Formula Student UK
- Started Egypt's electric vehicle competition legacy

### 2021 - Formula Student UK
- **Achievement:** Highest MENA rank in UK design event
- Continued electric vehicle development

### Recent Competitions
- Consistent participation in Formula Student events
- Focus on electric vehicle development and autonomous systems
