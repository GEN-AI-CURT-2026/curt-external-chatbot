# CURT General Information

## About Cairo University Racing Team (CURT)

### Who We Are
Cairo University Racing Team-Formula Student is a group of motivated students from Cairo University who are eager to get hands-on experience and valuable knowledge in engineering, business and teamwork in the automotive industry.

### What We Do
- We design, manufacture and compete a high-performance formula car every year, utilizing cutting-edge technology and engineering principles
- We participate in Formula Student competitions all over the world
- We are the 1st Egyptian Team to manufacture an electric formula car

### Mission
Through our project, we want to make the field of engineering more appealing to students so they would choose it as their future career. For engineering students, we give the possibility to get the best possible education through a practical experience of building a real formula student race car.

### History and Founding
- **Founded:** 2012 as a graduation project with Doctor Mostafa Shawky
- **First Competition:** Formula Student UK in 2012, making CURT the first Egyptian team and first team in the MENA region to participate in Formula Student
- **Pioneering Status:** Pioneer in Formula Student competitions not only in Egypt but also in Africa and the MENA Region

### Team Size

## About Formula Student Competition

Formula Student is the world's largest and most prestigious engineering competition, organized and run by the Institute of Mechanical Engineers. The competition attracts teams from all over the world to compete with a single-seater Formula-style car.

### Competition Statistics
- **12,000+** Active members globally
- **17+** Countries participating
- **100+** Partner organizations
- **30,000+** Experiences every year

### Competition Structure
The competition tests teams on:
- **Static Events:** Design judging, cost analysis, business presentation
- **Dynamic Events:** Acceleration, skid pad, autocross, endurance

### Rules and Requirements
- Teams must present vehicles fully completed before events (no fabrication allowed on site)
- Participants must be students at least 18 years old
- Drivers must hold a valid driver's license
- Points awarded across multiple categories
- Team with highest overall score in its class wins
- Emphasis on safety, professionalism, and innovation in engineering design
- Detailed requirements on suspension, engine limits, and body design depending on class

Formula Student is widely recognized as a premier educational motorsport event that prepares future engineers by combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience in a competitive environment.

## Recruitment

### Application Process
- Usually begins at start of academic year, immediately after competition (typically mid-August)
- Initial recruitment targets all subsystems
- Second round opens at end of January
- Additional in-season recruitment occurs at earliest convenient time
- All new members undergo mandatory learning phase before being fully immersed in design process

### How to Apply
- Follow Social Media Accounts and stay tuned for recruitment announcements
- Be passionate

### What It Takes to Get Accepted
Be passionate and willing to commit to learning and contributing.

## Time Commitment

Time commitment varies between subsystems and depends on goals set for the year:
- For some, participation equivalent to full-time job
- For others, more like part-time commitment
- Level of engineering work and dedication required is immense, demanding active and consistent involvement
- Members who are not actively contributing may receive warnings or be removed from team
- Maintaining high level of commitment is crucial for project success
